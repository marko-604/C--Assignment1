\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Derived class representing a Freezing \doxylink{class_tower}{Tower}. 

The \doxylink{class_freezing_tower}{Freezing\+Tower} slows down critters permanently while attacking them, at the cost of a higher purchase price.
