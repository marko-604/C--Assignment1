\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Derived class representing a Sniper \doxylink{class_tower}{Tower}. 

The \doxylink{class_sniper_tower}{Sniper\+Tower} offers better damage, longer range, and a higher hit rate compared to a regular tower, at the cost of a lower fire rate.
